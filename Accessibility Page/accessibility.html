Ensuring Accessibility for All

We have created this Accessibility Page with a strong commitment to making our website accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities or disabilities. Accessibility is a fundamental aspect of web development, and it aligns with our values of inclusivity and universal design. Our website has been tested for accessibility using automated testing tools to identify and address potential issues. The testing process involved checking for proper semantic HTML structure, ensuring meaningful text alternatives for non-text content, and verifying keyboard navigation functionality.
